Instruction

Concentration, also known as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexeso, or Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid facedown on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.

PG-Rated Fun for Everyone!

Although typically played as a single-player, there are tons of ways to get creative and involve your friends or whole family in this light-hearted brain teaser! It can be as fast or slow-paced and as high or low-risk as YOU want to play it.
